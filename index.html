Welcome to my academic homepage. I am a postdoctoral research fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, working with Prof. Jaafar El-Awady.

Broadly, my research focuses on integrating machine learning with multi-scale computational mechanics to accelerate the discovery of materials for extreme environments, energy, and environmental applications.

Research interests

  • Data-driven design of refractory multi-principal element alloys (RMPEAs)
  • Ice-nucleating proteins and mechanics of ice microstructures
  • Mechanically informed design of nanoporous and membrane materials
  • Machine learning surrogates for large-scale CALPHAD and atomistic simulations
