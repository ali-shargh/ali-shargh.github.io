I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Johns Hopkins University, where I conduct research on developing closed-loop automated laboratories for the accelerated discovery of refractory multi-principal element alloys (RMPEAs) for extreme operating environments. In parallel, my work focuses on modeling the thermo-mechanical behavior of ice and investigating ice-nucleation mechanisms to support emerging strategies aimed at slowing polar ice-sheet loss. I received my B.S. (2014) and M.S. (2017) in Mechanical Engineering from K. N. Toosi University of Technology and Sharif University of Technology, respectively, and my Ph.D. (2023) in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Rochester.
