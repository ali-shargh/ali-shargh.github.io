Ali Shargh, Ph.D.

News

  • September 2025: I was selected as a 2025 Rising Star in Materials Science and Engineering (MSE), co-organized by MIT, Stanford, CMU, and UIUC.

  • August 2025: A second paper from my postdoctoral research at JHU has been published in Computational Materials Science, presenting a machine-learning framework for discovering refractory multi-principal element alloys (RMPEAs) for extreme environments.

  • March 2025: I attended TMS 2025 in Las Vegas, where I presented a talk on my postdoctoral research.

  • January 2025: The first publication from my postdoctoral research at JHU has appeared in Acta Materialia, introducing a machine-learning framework for phase classification in refractory multi-principal element alloys (RMPEAs).

  • September 2024: I attended 11th International Conference on Multiscale Materials Modeling (MMM11) in Prague, Czech Republic and presented a talk and a poster on my current postdoctoral research at JHU.

  • April 2024: I attended the MACH 2024 Conference in Annapolis, and presented a talk on my current postdoctoral research at JHU.
